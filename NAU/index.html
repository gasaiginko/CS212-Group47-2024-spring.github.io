For Fresh Lumberjacks

Northern Arizona University (NAU), positioned at 1900 S Knoles Dr, Flagstaff, AZ 86011, boasts a sprawling campus that is distinctly divided into a southern and northern section by University Drive East, stretching between landmarks like Campus Height and Wilson Hall. This division allows for a well-organized space where academic buildings dedicated to a variety of disciplines, including mathematics, engineering, and computer science, are strategically located to cater to a diverse student body. NAU takes special care to ensure that its facilities, including those at Campus Height, are welcoming and equipped to meet the needs of international students, fostering a global learning environment.
To further enhance the collegiate experience, NAU offers a plethora of resources and amenities designed to support both academic and extracurricular activities. Among these is the Cline Library, which not only provides extensive collections and research materials but also features individual study rooms, group meeting spaces, computers, and large screens for presentations or collaborative projects. Complementing the academic facilities are comprehensive sports and recreation centers, including modern recreation centers, a swimming center, and climbing centers, which cater to the health and wellness needs of the NAU community.
